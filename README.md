# Naive Quiz

Hi! This is a super naive quiz app in an AI era. I made it because I just wanted a place to practice questions of courses/cert dumps and learn stuff without needing complicated servers, databases, or logins. Everything runs right inside your browser (cough, check indexeddb, cough).

### How it works
1. **You bring the questions:** You put your questions in a simple `.json` file. You can use an AI like Google's NotebookLM to magically turn your school notes or books into these files for you.
2. **You take the quiz:** You open the app, pick a topic, and start answering. It will test your knowledge and remember things using a smart flashcard system, so it shows you the hard questions more often.
3. **Ask an AI for help:** If you get stuck on a question and want to know *why* you got it wrong, there's an "Ask AI" button at the end. It doesn't secretly talk to robots in the background. Instead, it simply copies the question, your wrong answer, and the correct answer. You can then paste it into any AI you like (like ChatGPT, Gemini, or Claude, or even Claude Code) to ask for an explanation! I think this is better because it's free and you control it.

### Why is it called "Naive"?
Because it doesn't try to be overly smart or sneaky. All your data stays on your computer. If you have a folder of questions, you have a quiz app. It's just a simple tool built to help you learn!
